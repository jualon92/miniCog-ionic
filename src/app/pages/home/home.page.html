FAQ - Mini Cog
  • What is this?

    This is an app that will take you step by step to do the Mini Cog test with a loved one.

  • What is Mini Cog?

    The Mini Cog test is a brief cognitive screening test that is used to detect cognitive impairment in older adults. It consists of a three-item recall test for memory and a simple clock-drawing test.

  • Who is this for?

    This test is designed to be administered by one person to another, such as a family member or loved one.
