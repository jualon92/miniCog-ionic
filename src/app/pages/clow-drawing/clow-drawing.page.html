Clock Drawing Clock Drawing

Give the person a blank sheet of paper and a pen or pencil.

It is acceptable to provide a sheet of paper with the circle already drawn for the person.

Say: “Next, I want you to draw a clock for me. First, put in all of the numbers where they go.”

When that is completed, say: “Now, set the hands to 10 past 11.”

Repeat instructions as needed as this is not a memory test.
Move to Step 3 if the clock is not complete within three minutes.
