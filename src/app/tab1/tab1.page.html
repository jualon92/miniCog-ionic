The words are

  • Sunrise
  • Chair

After you have said all three words, ask the person to repeat them back to you.

Repeat the words if necessary, but do not provide any additional information or cues.

If the person is unable to repeat the words after three attempts, move on to Step 2 (clock drawing)..

Give the person a blank sheet of paper and a pen or pencil.

It is acceptable to provide a sheet of paper with the circle already drawn for the person.

Say: “Next, I want you to draw a clock for me. First, put in all of the numbers where they go.”

When that is completed, say: “Now, set the hands to 10 past 11.”

Repeat instructions as needed as this is not a memory test.
Move to Step 3 if the clock is not complete within three minutes.

  • Inability or refusal to draw a clock (abnormal) = 0 points.
Hand length is not scored.

Total score = Word Recall score + Clock Draw score.

  • A cut point of less than 3 on the Mini-Cog™ has been validated for dementia screening, but many individuals with clinically meaningful cognitive impairment will score higher.
  • When greater sensitivity is desired, a cut point of less than 4 is recommended as it may indicate a need for further evaluation of cognitive status.
